## Golf Course Web Scraping

## On-Board System for Driving Style Assesment
For my senior design project my team and I decided to build [the system](/pdf/poster.pdf) that would be capable of detecting aggresive driving behavior with further notification of the driver or parents (in cases, where the system was used as a parental control) by email or text messaging.

I was responsible for the driving style assessment using [accelerometer data](/pdf/PDR_Presentation_team25_acc.pdf). I used k-means clustering method to categorize actions: speeding, sharp turns, break slamming.
